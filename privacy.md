# PRIVACY POLICY — NARA
**Last Updated:** June 22, 2026  
**Effective Date:** June 22, 2026

## 1. INTRODUCTION
This Privacy Policy explains how Nara ("we", "our"), owned by Hams Dev, collects, uses, stores, and protects user data.

## 2. DATA WE COLLECT
- **User Info:** Discord ID, username, display name, avatar URL, nickname (AFK)
- **Ticket Data:** Message history, transcript HTML, timestamps, staff assignment
- **Moderation Data:** Warns, bans, timeouts, kicks with reasons and timestamps
- **Feedback Data:** Rating scores (1-5), feedback text, item names, receipt numbers
- **AFK Data:** Status, reason messages, previous nicknames, timestamps
- **Fun Data:** Interaction counters (slap, hug, etc.), target user IDs

## 3. HOW WE USE DATA
Data is used solely for Bot functionality. Data is NEVER sold, shared with advertisers, used for marketing, or monetized.

## 4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
All data is stored on a private, password-protected VPS. Only Hams Dev has server access. No third-party analytics or tracking.

## 5. DATA RETENTION
- Ticket transcripts: Deleted after 2 days
- Ticket history: Until manually cleared
- Moderation logs: Until manually cleared
- Rating data: Indefinitely (reset available)
- AFK data: Auto-removed on return or manual clear

## 6. DATA BREACH PROCEDURES
We will investigate, notify affected users within 72 hours, and secure systems.

## 7. USER RIGHTS
You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data. Contact us via email or Discord.

## 8. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
Nara uses Nekos.best (GIFs) and ImgBB (image upload). They have their own privacy policies.

## 9. COOKIES AND TRACKING
Nara does not use cookies or tracking.

## 10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Nara complies with Discord's 13+ age requirement.

## 11. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Data is stored in Indonesia. By using the Bot, you consent to this transfer.

## 12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
